01 — About me

The developer behind
the automation.

I'm Bilal Shabbir — a Power Platform Developer based in Birmingham, UK.

I started my career writing C# APIs, ERP modules, and stored procedures at Sumato Solutions — learning what enterprise software actually requires under the hood. When I joined Stallions Solutions, a Microsoft Gold Partner, Power Platform became my primary platform. I went deep on Dataverse schemas, Dynamics 365, Canvas and Model-Driven Apps, Azure integrations, and pro-code extensibility — shipping end-to-end solutions for healthcare, local government, and enterprise clients.

Now at Inteknix, I build the kind of automation that frees teams from manual work: a support ticket pipeline that eliminated 15 hours of weekly triage, a 3CX telephony reporting system with Azure Blob Storage and real-time dashboards, an AI Builder PO processing pipeline, and a contract management system wired to SQL Server, SharePoint, Azure, and Teams.

I hold the PL-600 Power Platform Solution Architect Expert certification — the highest-level Microsoft credential in this stack — alongside PL-400, PL-200, AZ-900, and Applied Skills certifications in Power Automate and Power Apps. Full right to work in the UK on a PSW Graduate Route Visa valid until March 2028. No sponsorship required.

I'm available for Power Platform development, SharePoint solutions, and consulting. This is where I document what I've learned the hard way.
