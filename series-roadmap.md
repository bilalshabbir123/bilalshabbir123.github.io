## Mr. YOLO — Character Brief

Recurring character across the series. Appears in every post making the mistake the post then fixes.

- Not a villain. A mirror.
- Fast, creative, well-intentioned — undisciplined.
- His mistakes are recognizable, not cartoonish.
- Tone: warm, lightly self-deprecating humor. Never condescending.
- Every post surfaces one "Mr. YOLO mistake" — the thing the post fixes.
